Welcome to Jewels & Pearls School

You are welcome to Jewels & Pearls School located in one of the most serene areas in Ogudu, Lagos.

Jewels & Pearls School is an Early Childhood Development Centre that was borne out of extreme passion for children, good value based education and grooming the total child.

Our environment is impeccably clean, cosy and comfortable. Our very spacious classes are conducive and equipped with the best of 21st century learning resources, well ventilated and air conditioned.

All these are consciously provided to enhance the overall physical and mental development of our pupils with no doubt that these will help them realise their potential and also develop positive attitude towards academics and Interpersonal skills.

Our Vision

To become a preferred learning destination for complete nurturing of future leaders through rigorous but sensitive development of the mind intellect and character.

Our Mission

Redefining early childhood education through character grooming and eclectic teaching approach to harness all innate and obvious potential of child.
